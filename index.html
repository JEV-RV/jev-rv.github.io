About this project

This guide series began as a personal project while planning a European adventure with our LTV Unity FX 2025 "Skøl." The research grew into a community resource for all North Americans considering RV travel in Europe — whether shipping their own rig, buying locally, renting, or co-owning.

No ads, no fees, no agenda. Built with rigour but without legal authority — always verify critical information with official sources before departure.

About the author

Jack-Éric Vandenbroucke — retired engineer, LTV Unity FX 2025 owner, compulsive planner and tinkerer. After 35 years in technical and analytical fields, planning a European motorhome adventure turned into building this community resource.

Questions and contributions welcome through the VR Europe Facebook group.
