Plan your multi-block Europe RV trip with correct rolling-window Schengen calculations and EU vehicle customs tracking.

🛂
YOUR clock — Schengen 90/180
You may stay max 90 days in any rolling 180-day window inside Schengen. Applies to you personally — your RV is irrelevant to this rule.
🚐
RV clock — EU Customs 6 months
Your North American-plated RV may stay max 6 months in EU customs territory. Separate from Schengen — moving between EU countries does not reset it.
Clean reset

Store outside EU customs

UK, Serbia, Montenegro, Albania — time outside EU customs territory resets both your Schengen counter and the RV customs clock simultaneously. The Balkans are the most efficient: one stop solves both.

Grey zone

France / Germany storage

Common in practice, but storing in France or Germany does NOT reset the EU vehicle customs clock. Fine if your total EU stay stays well under 6 months — risky if it approaches or exceeds it.
