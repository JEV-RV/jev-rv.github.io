Getting Started

Your complete guide to using the AI agents and interactive tools built for North American RV travel in Europe.

🤖 2 AI agents on ChatGPT 🌐 11 interactive tools 📊 Free to use
Step 1

Create your ChatGPT account first

The agents are hosted on ChatGPT. You need a free account to access them — no payment required to use the agents as a visitor.

1

Download the ChatGPT app

Search "ChatGPT" on the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). The official app is by OpenAI.

💡 You can also use ChatGPT in your browser at chat.openai.com — no app required.
2

Create a free account

Tap Sign up → use your email address or sign in with Google/Apple. The free plan is sufficient to access and use both agents.

🔒 The agent creator (Jack-Eric Vandenbroucke) uses a Pro account to host the agents — you only need a free account to use them.
3

Open the agents via the links above

Click Open Concierge or Open Sentinel above — it will open directly in ChatGPT. On mobile, it may ask you to open in the app.

4

Pin the agents to your sidebar

Once the agent is open, right-click on the agent name at the top left of the screen (or long-press on mobile) → select "Pin to sidebar" (or "Keep in sidebar"). The agent will then always be accessible from the left panel.

💡 Do this for both Concierge and Sentinel so you can switch between them in one tap while on the road.
Step 2

Meet the 2 AI agents

Two specialized agents built from 11 guides and 200+ pages of field-validated RV Europe knowledge. They complement each other — one plans your trip, the other fixes your RV.

Pro tip

The constraints checklist

Copy-paste this into Concierge at the start of a planning session to get the most relevant answers. Fill in the brackets with your own details.

Ready-to-use prompt template
I travel in a [brand/model] motorhome, [height] m high, [length] m long, [weight] tons.

I want a [duration]-day itinerary in Europe, max [km] km/day, [hours] hours driving max, [rhythm] style (e.g. 4-4-4).

I want to avoid: [tolls / big cities / ZTL zones / narrow roads / ferries]
I prefer: [authentic villages / nature / panoramas / local markets / beaches]
Season: [month/season] — Budget: ~[€/day] for [number] people

Give me a concept itinerary + 2 variants + a list of what to verify in Park4Night.
Example — with a dog
I'm RVing with a dog. I want a 21-day itinerary, max 150 km/day, 3 hours driving max. Avoid big cities, ZTL zones, narrow roads, and places where dogs are not allowed. I prefer accessible beaches, hiking trails, quiet villages and dog-friendly campsites. My RV is 3m high and 7m long. Give me a concept itinerary + points to check in Park4Night for dog-friendly areas.
